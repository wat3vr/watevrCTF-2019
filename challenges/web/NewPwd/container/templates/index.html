NewPwd

A new, revolutionary log-in method.

Get Started

Logging in has never been more secure.

The biggest weakness with login systems used to be that if you leaked your password anyone could log into your account. Some sites added 2 factor authentication to combat this, only making their sites more clunky to use. With NewPwd, you have a different password for each device making leaking your password a non-issue!

Automatically generated passwords

Each time you attempt to log-in from a new device a new password will be automatically generated for you. This password only works on that device, so if someone steals your password but not your device it will be useless to them!

Zero tolerance invalid CAPTCHA policy

Every time you try to log-in you will need to complete a CAPTCHA to protect against automated bots. We here at NewPwd have a zero tolerance policy against incorrectly completed captchas, and will block all log-in attempts from devices which have sent an incorrect captcha in the past, blocking away brute forcing bots entierly!
